## Observations & Issues

### 1. Header missing funding rate indicator
The header shows raw "Funds: $1000" with no income/burn rate indicator. When policies are active, the player has no way to see their net funding rate without mental math. A "(+$5/t)" or "(-$5/t)" indicator would help players understand their financial trajectory at a glance.

### 2. Help panel could be more informative
The Help panel only lists key bindings. It doesn't explain gameplay concepts like:
- What research does or the research pipeline (Identify → Develop → Clinical Trial)
- What policies do or their trade-offs
- How funding/RP/personnel work
- What the severity indicators (OK/LOW/MOD/HIGH/CRIT) mean

### 3. No event log or history
Mutation events appear at the bottom of the screen but are transient — if you miss it, the information is gone. There's no event log panel to review past events.

### 4. Game balance observation
By tick 100, Asia has 104.7K dead out of ~4.5B total pop. The player hasn't been able to take any meaningful action yet because:
- No medicines available (need research first)
- Research requires RP (earned at +1/tick)
- First identify costs 10 RP = 10 ticks of waiting
- Then 20 ticks to complete = tick 30 earliest for first identify
- Then need knowledge ≥ 0.75 for medicine dev = second identify needed
- Full pipeline to first usable medicine: ~80-100+ ticks minimum
This creates a long "waiting period" where the player watches diseases spread with nothing to do except enable policies.

## Verdict
Core game loop is functional. All panels render correctly, navigation works, research/policy systems are accessible. The main UX gap is the lack of a funding rate indicator in the header — the player needs to mentally calculate whether they're gaining or losing money.
